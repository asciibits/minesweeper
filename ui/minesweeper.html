Board Layout ?
There are three "standard" modes:
  • Expert: 30x16 grid with 99 mines
  • Intermediate: 16x16 grid with 40 mines
  • Beginner: 16x16 grid with 10 mines
You can enter any combinations of width/height/mine-count you prefer but be advised that any mine count over 25% of all cells tends to lead to an unsolvable game.
Initial click ?
This defines what happens on your first click to a new board.
  • No Mine: The first click will not be a mine.
  • Open Area: The first click will be an open area.
  • No Guess: (Not implemented yet) The first click will be an open area, and it is guaranteed that the game can be solved without guessing.
  • Possible Mine: The first click could be anything, including a mine.
